What is Vibe Coding?

Companies today want speed and insight, not just lines of code. Vibe Coding is the modern workflow of using AI to accelerate analysis.

We teach you how to "Manage" the code rather than "Write" every character. You provide the business logic and the prompt; the AI handles the syntax. This makes you 10x faster than a traditional analyst.
